# 💡 R&D Explorer Skill (The "Idea Scout")

This skill allows an AI subagent to act as a general researcher. It ensures the "Spider Bubble" is always looking for new ways to optimize the terminal or find alpha.

## Monitoring Goals
1.  **Yield Exploration**: Finding high-APR, "Clean" DeFi yield opportunities (DefiLlama / Hive data).
2.  **Tool Discovery**: Scanning GitHub and X for new open-source trading indicators or AI mods.
3.  **Protocol Scanning**: Identifying "V3" or "V4" updates in major exchanges that allow new trading strategies.

## Reporting Logic
When a new idea is discovered:
1.  **Discovery Pitch**: Submit a "Research Item" to the **Notion Brainstorm** session.
2.  **Logic Logic**: Summarize the "Edge" (e.g. "This new tool catches liquidations 10s earlier than TV").
3.  **Wait for OK**: No development begins without "ADMIN OK" status.

## Extensibility
*   **Slack**: Post weekly "Alpha Summaries" to `#brainstorm`.
*   **Notion**: Maintain the "Universal Research Registry" for all version-controlled ideas.
